Adobe Garamond Pro / Regular

Adobe Garamond Pro was designed by Robert Slimbach in 1989. It was designed as a digital interpretation of the roman types of Claude Garamond and the italic types of Robert Granjon (History). Slimbach captured the beauty and balance of the original Garamond typefaces but also offered all the advantages of a contemporary digital type family (Adobe Garamond Pro). Its rich history, elegance and beauty are the reason why I chose this font.
